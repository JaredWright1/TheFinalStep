Chicken With Bamboo Shoots

This dish is an all-time favorite in chinese resturants. Here it is made by the authenic method, with the ingredients cut into small, neat slices, not tiny shreads

    Serves 4
  • 6-8 dried Chinese Mushrooms, soaked (page 63)
  • 1/2 lb skinless and boneless chicken breast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp lightly Beaten egg white
  • 2 tsps cornstarch mixed with 1 tb water
  • 2 Cups Vegatable Oil
